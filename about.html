Our Story

Built on Trust & Hard Work

FIXED Plumbing Services was founded in 2025 with one simple goal: to bring professional, reliable, and affordable plumbing services to Mowe, Redemption Camp, and the wider Ogun State community.

We saw how many homeowners and businesses struggled to find trustworthy plumbers — people who show up on time, do the job right, and charge a fair price. So we built FIXED to be exactly that company.

Based right here in Redemption Camp, we know the terrain, the water systems, and the specific plumbing challenges our community faces. That local knowledge makes us faster and more effective than anyone else.
